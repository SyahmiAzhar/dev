    The Sistem Smart Inisiatif Peduli Rakyat (SSIPR) is designed to streamline the management of user data for the People's Initiative (IPR) programs, centralizing everything in one convenient location. Initiated by the Selangor State Government, the IPR aims to support the community, particularly low-income groups, in navigating the demands and challenges of modern life. SSIPR allows individuals to submit their applications online, eliminating the need for in-person visits to government offices. Additionally, it helps users discover which IPR programs they are eligible for and simplifies the process of checking application statuses, making it more efficient and user-friendly.
