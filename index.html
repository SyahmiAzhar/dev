The IPR introduced by the Selangor State Government is aimed at helping the community, especially the low-income group, meet the demands and challenges of today's increasingly challenging life. The SSIPR was created to enable the people to submit their applications online without having to present themselves to the relevant offices. People can also find out the IPR agenda that is worth applying for and make reviewing your application status easier and more time-consuming.
